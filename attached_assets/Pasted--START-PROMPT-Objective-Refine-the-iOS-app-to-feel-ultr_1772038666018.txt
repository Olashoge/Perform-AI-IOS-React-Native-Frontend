-----------------------------------

- Save to backend at completion

Step 2 – Goal

- Target weight (optional)

Step 3 – Training (required before plans)
- Experience
- Activity level

Step 4 – Nutrition (skippable)

- Foods to avoid

After completion → route to Dashboard.

Implement restrained institutional color system.

Brand Ink (Primary Dark): #0B1220
Background: #F6F7FB
Card: #FFFFFF
Border: #E7EBF2
Secondary Text: #5B667A
Primary Accent (Deep Blue): #2D5BFF

Status Colors (muted, not saturated):
Success: #2E8B57
Warning: #D08A1D
Risk: #B65C5C

Rules:
- Only Brand Ink and Deep Blue are used for primary actions.
- Status colors are muted and only used for indicators.

- Desaturate all icon background colors by ~30%.
